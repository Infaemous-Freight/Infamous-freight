## 🎬 Video Production Tips

### Screen Recording Best Practices
1. **Clean Interface:** Hide personal information, bookmarks, notifications
2. **High Resolution:** Record at 1920x1080 minimum
3. **Smooth Mouse Movement:** Don't rush, move cursor deliberately
4. **Prepared Demo Data:** Use realistic but sanitized data
5. **Multiple Takes:** Record each section multiple times

### Narration Tips
1. **Script First:** Write and approve script before recording
2. **Professional Mic:** Use good quality microphone
3. **Quiet Environment:** No background noise
4. **Pacing:** Speak clearly, not too fast
5. **Retakes:** Don't be afraid to re-record

### Editing Tips
1. **Jump Cuts:** Cut out pauses, mistakes, "ums"
2. **B-Roll:** Use B-roll to cover cuts
3. **Captions:** Always add captions
4. **Brand Consistency:** Use brand colors, fonts, logo
5. **Call to Action:** End every video with clear next steps
