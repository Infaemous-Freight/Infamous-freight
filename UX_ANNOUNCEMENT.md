## 💡 Top Tips from Our Power Users

1. **Master Search:** Use `⌘K` constantly. Search for anything - it's faster than clicking through menus.

2. **Go Vim-Style:** Press `g` then another key for quick jumps. `gh` = home, `gd` = dashboard.

3. **Keep Help Open:** Press `⌘/` to open help in a corner window while you work.

4. **Mobile Swipe:** Swipe left/right to navigate between tabs on mobile - it's natural!

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need to do anything to access these features?**  
A: No! Everything is already enabled. Just refresh your browser or restart the app.

**Q: Will this work on my device?**  
A: Yes! Works on all modern browsers and mobile devices (iOS/Android).

**Q: What if I prefer the old way?**  
A: The core functionality is the same, we've just made it faster and easier. Give it a try - we think you'll love it!

**Q: Can I turn off keyboard shortcuts?**  
A: Keyboard shortcuts only activate when you use them. They won't interfere with normal typing in forms.

**Q: How do I learn all the shortcuts?**  
A: Press `?` anytime to see the complete list. Start with just `⌘K` for search and add more as you go!

**Q: Is my data safe with these changes?**  
A: Absolutely! These are UI improvements only. Your data security remains our top priority.

**Q: Where can I give feedback?**  
A: We'd love to hear from you! Click `?` → "Send Feedback" or email feedback@infamousfreight.com
