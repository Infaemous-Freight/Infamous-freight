# Delaware C-Corp Setup Checklist

> Informational only. This is not legal or tax advice.

## Status Summary

- Entity type: Delaware C-Corp
- Ownership: 100%
- Cap table: Clean (100%)

## Formation Checklist

1. Certificate of Incorporation filed with Delaware.
2. EIN issued by the IRS.
3. Registered agent active in Delaware.
4. Initial board consent and officer appointments.
5. Bylaws adopted and stored in the records book.

## Governance & Records

- Cap table created and maintained (including issued shares).
- Stock ledger and issuance docs (stock purchase or subscription agreements).
- IP assignment to the corporation from founders/contractors.
- Annual franchise tax and report calendar tracked.

## Banking & Compliance

- Business bank account opened under the corporation.
- Accounting system configured with entity-level chart of accounts.
- State and local registrations reviewed for operating jurisdictions.

## Equity & Compensation

- Equity plan drafted and approved (if issuing options).
- 83(b) election process documented for early equity grants.
- Offer letters and contractor agreements aligned to corporate entity.

## Ongoing Tasks

- Delaware franchise tax and annual report filed each year.
- Maintain good standing certificate as needed for financing, banking, or sales.
- Board minutes and material resolutions archived.
