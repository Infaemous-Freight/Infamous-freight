## Slide 2 - Problem

Freight operators lose margin due to:

- Fragmented tools across dispatch, billing, and compliance
- Manual workflows and limited visibility
- Pricing volatility and unreliable carrier data

The core issue is operational fragmentation. Most fleets and brokers run on a patchwork of legacy systems, spreadsheets, and point tools, which creates blind spots in cost, service, and compliance. That fragmentation compounds as volume scales, shrinking margins and slowing decisions that directly impact profitability and customer experience.

## Slide 3 - Solution

One platform to unify freight operations:

- Dispatch automation
- Real-time profitability and billing controls
- AI-assisted decisioning
- Compliance-ready workflows

Position the solution as a single system of record and action that replaces disjointed tools. The platform turns operational data into actionable workflows, with AI handling routine decisions and surfacing exceptions for human review, so teams move faster with fewer errors.

## Slide 4 - Product

## Visual Direction Guide (Slide-by-Slide)

Overall feel: industrial precision + modern AI. Clean, confident, data-forward. Use restrained color with bold accent. Keep layouts airy with strong grid alignment.

Core palette (example):
- Deep slate: #0F172A
- Steel: #334155
- Signal orange: #F97316
- Fog: #E2E8F0
- White: #FFFFFF

Typography (example):
- Display: IBM Plex Sans Condensed (titles)
- Body: IBM Plex Sans (copy, captions)
- Data: IBM Plex Mono (metrics)

Textures and imagery:
- Subtle industrial textures (thin grid, halftone, or map lines)
- Freight imagery: trailers, ports, warehouses, route overlays
- Use monochrome photos with a single accent overlay
