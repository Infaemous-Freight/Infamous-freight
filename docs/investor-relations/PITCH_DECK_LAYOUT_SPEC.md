# Pitch Deck Layout Spec (16:9)

Purpose: concrete layout rules for building slides with consistent hierarchy, spacing, and visual rhythm.

## Canvas
- Size: 1920 x 1080 (16:9)
- Safe margins: 96 px on all sides
- Baseline grid: 8 px
- Columns: 12
- Gutter: 24 px
- Max text width: 8 columns (avoid full-width paragraphs)

## Global Zones
- Header band: top 160 px (title, short subtitle)
- Body band: 160 px to 880 px (primary content)
- Footer band: bottom 120 px (source, contact, footnotes)

## Spacing Rules
- Section spacing: 48 px between major blocks
- Item spacing: 24 px between related elements
- Tight spacing: 16 px for labels and captions
- Avoid more than 3 text blocks per slide

## Typography Placement
- Slide title: left aligned, row 1 of header band
- Subtitle: directly under title, max 2 lines
- Metric labels: all-caps, 12-14 px
- Metrics: large numeric lockups in body band

## Visual Hierarchy
- One dominant focal element per slide (chart, diagram, or hero image)
- Secondary elements support the focal point, never compete
- Tertiary elements are annotations or footnotes

## Component Layouts
- Metric card: 1 column label + 2 column value; 16 px inner padding
- Chart block: 6-8 columns width; 360-420 px height
- Diagram block: 6-8 columns width; centered in body band
- Image block: 12 columns, 700-900 px height for hero

---

## Slide-by-Slide Layout

Slide 1 - Title
- Full-bleed hero image with overlay
- Title left, top third; subtitle below
- Small contact row in footer band

Slide 2 - Problem
- 3-column grid of pain points
- Each column: icon, short headline, 1-line support
- Background grid texture at 10% opacity

Slide 3 - Solution
- Centered hub-and-spoke diagram (8 columns wide)
- One-line benefit statement below
- Minimal text in footer band

Slide 4 - Product
- 2x2 tile grid across 10 columns
- Each tile: icon, module name, micro copy
- Optional UI thumbnails in each tile

Slide 5 - Market
- Left: TAM/SAM/SOM stacked bars (6 columns)
- Right: small North America map (4 columns)
- TAM label aligned to top of body band

Slide 6 - Traction
- Top row: 4 metric cards across 12 columns
- Bottom: growth chart spanning 10 columns
- Caption and source in footer band

Slide 7 - Unit Economics
- Left column: LTV/CAC, CAC, LTV (4 columns)
- Right column: bar or waterfall chart (6 columns)
- Payback callout chip near chart

Slide 8 - Business Model
- Vertical stack diagram (6 columns)
- Right side: revenue expansion arrow + 2 bullets
- Keep labels short and bold

Slide 9 - Go-To-Market
- Centered flywheel (7 columns)
- Three labels around wheel, small captions below
- Footer band: partner logos in a single row

Slide 10 - Competitive Landscape
- 2x2 matrix (8 columns) centered
- Highlight Infæmous Freight with ring or glow
- Competitor labels outside plot area

Slide 11 - Moat
- Three vertical pillars, equal width
- Each pillar: icon + 1-line proof
- Darker background to signal defensibility

Slide 12 - Financial Plan
- 3-year bar chart (8 columns) centered
- Milestone badges above bars
- Footnote assumptions in footer band

Slide 13 - Team
- Row of headshots (3-4 max)
- Under each: name, role, 1-line proof
- Optional prior logos in footer band

Slide 14 - The Ask
- Left: raise amount lockup (5 columns)
- Right: use-of-funds donut (5 columns)
- Bottom: milestone timeline bar

Slide 15 - Closing
- Full-bleed hero or abstract map lines
- Single closing statement in center-left
- Contact row in footer band
