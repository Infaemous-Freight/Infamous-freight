# Investor Outreach Strategy

## Target Investor Profile

- Stage: Series A or late seed
- Focus: B2B SaaS, logistics tech, supply chain, fintech
- Check size: $5M to $25M
- Location: US based with follow-on capacity

## Outreach Sequence (4 Weeks)

### Week 1 - Preparation

- Finalize deck, metrics, and data room
- Build investor list with tiers (A, B, C)
- Gather warm intro paths

### Week 2 - Warm Introductions

- Target 10 to 15 warm intros
- Schedule 5 to 8 first calls
- Track objections and refine the deck

### Week 3 - Momentum

- Expand to 25 to 30 total meetings
- Share a concise update memo weekly
- Create competitive tension with clear timeline

### Week 4 - Term Sheet Push

- Focus on lead investor conversion
- Align on valuation and governance terms
- Drive to term sheet with firm deadline

## Outreach Materials Checklist

- Investor deck (PDF)
- One-page executive summary
- Data room index
- Metrics dashboard (ARR, MRR, churn, CAC, LTV)
- Customer references and case studies

## Call Agenda (30 Minutes)

- 5 min: Founder background
- 10 min: Problem and solution
- 10 min: Traction and metrics
- 5 min: Round details and next steps

## Success Metrics

- 10 to 15 investor meetings in 2 weeks
- 2 to 3 partners in active diligence
- 1 lead investor by week 4
