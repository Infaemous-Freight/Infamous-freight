# Security Policy

## Vulnerability Reporting Process

If you discover a vulnerability in this repository, please report it to us as soon as possible. We take security issues seriously and strive to address them promptly. Follow the steps below to report a vulnerability:

1. **Send an Email**: Email your findings to [security@infamousfreight.com](mailto:security@infamousfreight.com).
2. **Provide Detailed Information**: Include as much detail as possible about the vulnerability, including:
   - A description of the vulnerability.
   - Steps to reproduce the issue.
   - Any screenshots or logs that may help us understand the problem.
3. **Wait for a Response**: We aim to respond to all reported vulnerabilities within 48 hours. We will work with you to resolve the issue in a timely manner.

## Contact Details

For security-related inquiries, please contact us at [security@infamousfreight.com](mailto:security@infamousfreight.com). We appreciate your help in keeping our project secure and your cooperation in responsibly disclosing vulnerabilities.

Thank you for helping to keep our repositories secure!
