## 💰 REVENUE PROJECTIONS & ROI

### Conservative Scenario (Year 1)

**Assumptions**:

- 500 total customers by end of year
- 50% Starter, 30% Professional, 20% Enterprise
- 15% monthly churn
- $500 CAC (customer acquisition cost)
- 70% gross margin

**Customer Distribution**:

```
250 × Starter @ $79/mo  = $19,750/month
150 × Professional @ $199/mo = $29,850/month
100 × Enterprise @ $600/mo = $60,000/month
────────────────────────────────────────
Total MRR = $109,600
Annual = $1,315,200
```

**Costs**:

- Infrastructure: $15,000/month
- Support staff: $20,000/month
- Marketing: $10,000/month
- Operations: $5,000/month
- **Total: $50,000/month**

**Gross Profit**: $59,600/month ($715,200/year)  
**Gross Margin**: 54%
